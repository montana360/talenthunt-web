We are about talent!

Talent Hunt is the first platform to marry features of TV talent competitions, video sharing portals and mobile payment platforms to establish a self-reinforcing talent discovery model. We are a group of people passionate about music; and as such, are focused on building an online-music platform, connecting talented singers with their much needed audience and access to the Talent Hunt competition. This will enable the discovery of great talent, whilst offering access to record deals and an amazing audience.


We Offer:

Exposure

The Talent Hunt social network achieves this by helping our participants develop their social network and audience reach for their craft.

Access

By joining the Talent Hunt competition, participants stand the opportunity to win a one year record label contract to push their career to higher heights.
