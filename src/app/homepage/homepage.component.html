Talent Hunt 2020

It is that time of the year again, where everyone wants to be heard, their craft acknowledged and their talent acclaimed. Your opportunity is here! Register and join the maiden edition of the Talent Hunt 2020 Music Competition to stand the chance of winning a GHS 100,000 cash prize, inclusive of a one year record label deal.

Don't just read and gasp in awe! Make that move. Right here. Right Now. Register and upload a personal original demo or a music cover to stand the chance of being selected join the competition.

It is time to tell your story! #TalentHunt2020

Description

Like all talent competitions on our traditional television screens, the Talent Hunt competition would hold a preliminary auditioning stage. Interested participants would have to submit their audition videos via the Talent Hunt mobile app on their smartphones, after successful purchase and completion of registration forms also via the app. Competition videos should be either a personal demo or a cover.

After successful auditions, twelve (12) competitors shall be selected by our three (3) resident judges – Reggie Rockstone, Richie Mensah and Okyeame Kwame – to partake in the maiden edition of the Talent Hunt competition. With a final four (4) competitors for the grand finale of the Talent Hunt 2020 Competition.

The Talent Hunt competition would be held over a sixteen-week (16 weeks) duration spanning September to December 2020. The twelve selected competitors shall be tasked with weekly activities by the judges to explore their musical talents and craft. There shall be surprise judges for each week’s online show on the Talent Hunt app. After each weekly tasks, following votes received and feedback from the Judges, one or more competitors shall be evicted and shall not participate in the next round of the Talent Hunt 2020 Music Competition.

Each week’s competition shall be streamed live on the Talent Hunt app, in partnership with Zoom. Judges’ comments and feedback for each competitor’s performance shall be via the app.

Awards: Ultimate Prize

A GHC 100,000.00 cash prize, inclusive of a one year record label for the Winner.e
